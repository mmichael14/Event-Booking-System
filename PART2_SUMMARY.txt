====================================================================
PART 2 - DATA AND DATA STRUCTURES
====================================================================
This is where the raw data exists and where the two data structures 
(Priority Queue and Binary Search Tree) are used by the whole
website are defined:
  - EVENTS_DATA: (not a data structure) this is just an array holding
    every event (title, category, date,isoDate, time, venue, price, 
    seats available(whether it's a VIP or a standard)).
    In terms of the CRUD operations (Create,Read,Update and Delete) 
    or to add/edit/remove an event it all happens here.
  - PriorityQueue: In here whoever has the highest internal
    "priority" number gets served first. VIP bookings and standard
    bookings live in separate number ranges so that VIPs always outrank
    standard users (explained fully in Part 4).
  - BinarySearchTree (BSTNode): organizes events by price so
    they can be searched/sorted, selected and booked efficiently by the
    user.

This is the crucial part of the whole website coz it holds the structures 
responsible for creating, viewing, editing and deleting of data and events.
Part 2 is connected with Part 3,4 and 5 .
