====================================================================
PART 7 - BOOKING CONFIRMATION
====================================================================
Everything that happens after the user clicks "Confirm Booking":
  - confirmBooking(): validates the checkout form the (name/email/phone
    is filled in, a valid email and a valid phone number length) which
    builds the actual booking object and adds it to the PriorityQueue,
    the myTickets tab and triggers the confirmation popup.
  - formatDDMMYYYY(): turns an event's isoDate ("2026-07-25") into the
    "25/07/2026" format shown on the PDF.
  - showConfirmation(): builds the "Booking Confirmed!" popup showing
    all the booking details (event, venue, date, time, seat(s), name,
    payment method, total) with Download PDF and the Close button.
  - closeConfirmation(): closes the popup.
  - downloadConfirmation(): uses the jsPDF library (loaded in Part 1's
    <head>) to generate and download a real PDF ticket.

This part is just the whole checkout process to ticket download.

INITIALIZATION SECTION 

These lines kicks off everything after the booking confirmation page loads by 
calling renderEvents() and renderQueue() so the page isn't empty or unresponsive
plus a console.log() lines for debugging the whole website incase of major errors.
